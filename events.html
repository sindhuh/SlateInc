Redlands Youth Film Festival


The Redlands Youth Film Festival will feature film and media artists currently enrolled in K-12 grade schools for the 2015-2016 school year. Participants will be able to screen their work, discuss their process, and meet with industry professionals. At the end of the screenings there will be an awards ceremony. Awards include a grand jury award. best director, best screenplay, and best actor. Prizes may include filmmaking tools and or scholarships for further education in the arts and craft of film and media. All film projects must be under 20 minutes in length.

To Submit complete the form below with payment. Films can be submitted electronically or mail. If you submit electronically you can use dropbox info@slate-inc.org to send a DVD copy of the film through mail send to Slate Inc., PO Box 7232, Redlands CA, 92375.


Students in San Bernardino or Riverside County have no charge to submit films

Early bird submission fee March 1, 2016 to April 30, 2016 – $10

Regular submission fee May 1, 2016 to June 1, 2016 – $20

Payment methods include check or money orders made out to SLATE Inc. or paypal
