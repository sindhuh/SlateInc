Slate Inc partners with The Guild an Arts Collective based in Redlands California and Masonic Lodge for this quarterly event. This event provides local and first time film artists an opportunity to show their work. This event will feature classic film screenings with talks to follow after the screening. Finally, it is a celebration of another art form here in the Inland Empire, beer. Each event will spotlight a different local brewery and food pairing.

Event Schedule is as follows for 2016:
(All screenings occur at the Masonic Lodge of Redlands, 131 Cajon St., Redlands CA, 92373)
  • August 10, 2016 7pm to 10pm
  • November 9, 2016 7pm to 10pm
