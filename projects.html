Youth Film Education Programs
Any youth between Kindergarten to Senior in high school may participate in our programs. We teach the basic principles of film and media arts. Students will learn how to develop a story, write a script, shoot the film, and complete it in post-production.

San Bernardino Documentary Project
Slate Inc. is in the process of telling the story of San Bernardino. A once vital city in the Inland Empire area, that’s been taken over by gangs, poverty, and most recently in the news for a horrific Terrorist Attack in 2015. Future Projects
The focus of this project is to provide education in the arts and craft of film making to youth across the region in Downtown San Bernardino. Primarily it will be open to students during after school and hours and the summer. Our vision is a state of the art facility that provides the tools necessary to teach future filmmakers as well as provide tools to current Filmmakers who film the Inland Empire. Instead of filming here and running back to LA they can finish their project here in the IE thereby creating jobs for our region.
